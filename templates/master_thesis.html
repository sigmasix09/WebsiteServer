Master thesis at Siemens Energy AB

Topic: Nearest Neighbour Search Method For 3D Data Mapping of CFD Data. The thesis was performed at the Mechanical Integrity sub-department in Combustion Research and Development from January to June 2024. The essence of the thesis revolves around the efficient handling of large CFD data and development of search algorithm methodology using the subroutine approach to perform data mapping. The aim was to reduce the simulation time for detailed Heat Transfer Analysis using this approach.


Thesis Report LinkedIn      LiU Publication     

The thesis work was performed using ABAQUS software and the FORTRAN programming language to carry out the heat transfer analysis using CFD data. Promising results were achieved with the two new methodologies developed during the thesis work. The methodologies were able to reduces the simulation time significantly for both single-core and multi-core analysis on the computer cluster.
