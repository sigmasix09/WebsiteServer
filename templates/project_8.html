Quadcopter Design

The Quadcopter, it was an idea by one of my friends to design a quadcopter that can fly and operate under water too. This design allowed me to cater the user requirements, user being my friends and I was designing it as per his requirements.


The requirements felt simple in the first hour of the conversation, also I was designing it in parallel with conversation. But after the first hour the technicalities started to compound gradually. I noted down the requirements on a piece of paper and went home to design it accordingly. The pictures above were the end result of that design which I gave to my friend next day.
