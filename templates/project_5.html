Aircraft Wing Design Project

An Aeronautical Engineer starts learning Aerodynamics from the airfoil geometry. The wing and the aerodynamics around it is the essence of Aeronautical Engineering. The wing is a vital part that performs various functions and is embedded with complex mechanisms.


This project in particular took considerable amount of time for me to completed it. It was similar to an independent mini project where I was required to complete my literature survey first. Secondly, I started designing the wing using various available options provided in CATIA. The time arrived where I had to scale down & project the airfoil sketch on the offset planes at regular intervals. Eventually, the project got completed and I felt like "I got the wings, now its time to fly".
