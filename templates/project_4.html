Jet Engine Design Project

Creating a complete Jet Engine Assembly is quit task. So many parts to be designed and assembled gives you the chills. Putting theoretical knowledge to practical is what I learned while doing this project.


This project was designed using CATIA V5. It was one of the projects in the miscellaneous project booklet. This booklet contained various complex design projects that test the skill of an individual in the CATIA certification course. From reading theories about air breathing engines to design to design each part of a jet engine in CATIA was quite a challenge and an achievement for me.
